About

Designing spaces where form and feeling speak the same language.

I am an architecture student with a strong academic foundation, having completed a Diploma in Architecture. My education has provided me with a balanced understanding of architectural theory, construction technology, and practical design application.

I am passionate about designing meaningful spaces for living beings that integrate functionality, comfort, and aesthetics. I have hands-on experience in architectural drawings, space planning, form development, and visualization through academic projects.

From the fluid campus geometry of The Flow Forum to the careful heritage documentation of Udvada's Parsi dwellings — my work explores the boundary between the structural and the spiritual.
